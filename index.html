Parafin er en token på Binance Smart Chain med et mål om å gjøre deg rik på kunsten du selger, eller bare ved å holde den.

Slik kjøper du Parafin

Last ned og konfigurer MetaMask eller TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Kjøp og send BNB til MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

Gå over til PancakeSwap og Swap for Parafin

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address 0xef2ec90e0b8d4cdfdb090989ea1bc663f0d680bf to select Orfano. Set the slippage tolerance to 3% (on error you may change it up to 4%, if that doesn't fix the error, change the Orfano output amount to end with a "0" as the last digit [i.e. 12345.123 => 12340])

Se og HODL Parafin

Swap BNB for Orfano. Now you need to add Orfano to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) to view your Orfano. Lastly HODL!
